## [cite_start]01 PROJECT OVERVIEW [cite: 10]
[cite_start]The platform is a subscription-driven web application combining golf performance tracking, charity fundraising, and a monthly draw-based reward engine[cite: 11]. [cite_start]It is designed to feel emotionally engaging and modern - deliberately avoiding the aesthetics of a traditional golf website[cite: 12].

[cite_start]Users will: [cite: 13]
* [cite_start]Subscribe to the platform (monthly or yearly) [cite: 14]
* [cite_start]Enter their latest golf scores in Stableford format [cite: 14]
* [cite_start]Participate in monthly draw-based prize pools [cite: 14]
* [cite_start]Support a charity of their choice with a portion of their subscription [cite: 15]

## [cite_start]05 SCORE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM [cite: 42]
[cite_start]**Input Requirements** [cite: 43]
* [cite_start]Users must enter their last 5 golf scores [cite: 44]
* [cite_start]Score range: 1-45 (Stableford format) [cite: 45]
* [cite_start]Each score must include a date [cite: 46]

[cite_start]**Functional Behaviour** [cite: 47]
* [cite_start]Only the latest 5 scores are retained at any time [cite: 48]
* [cite_start]A new score replaces the oldest stored score automatically [cite: 49]
* [cite_start]Scores are displayed in reverse chronological order (most recent first) [cite: 50]

## [cite_start]06 DRAW & REWARD SYSTEM [cite: 51]
[cite_start]**Draw Types** [cite: 52]
* [cite_start]5-Number Match [cite: 53]
* [cite_start]4-Number Match [cite: 54]
* [cite_start]3-Number Match [cite: 55]

[cite_start]**Draw Logic Options** [cite: 56]
* [cite_start]Random generation: standard lottery-style draw [cite: 57, 58]
* [cite_start]Algorithmic - weighted by most/least frequent user scores [cite: 59]

[cite_start]**Operational Requirements** [cite: 60]
* [cite_start]Monthly cadence draws executed once per month [cite: 61]
* [cite_start]Admin controls publishing of draw results [cite: 62]
* [cite_start]Simulation/pre-analysis mode before official publish [cite: 63]
* [cite_start]Jackpot rollover to next month if no 5-match winner [cite: 63]

## [cite_start]07 PRIZE POOL LOGIC [cite: 68]
A fixed portion of each subscription contributes to the prize pool. [cite_start]Distribution is pre-defined and enforced automatically. [cite: 69]
* [cite_start]**5-Number Match:** 40% (Yes, Jackpot) [cite: 70]
* [cite_start]**4-Number Match:** 35% (No Jackpot) [cite: 70]
* [cite_start]**3-Number Match:** 25% (No Jackpot) [cite: 70]
* [cite_start]Auto-calculation of each pool tier based on active subscriber count [cite: 71]
* [cite_start]Prizes split equally among multiple winners in the same tier [cite: 72]
* [cite_start]5-Match jackpot carries forward if unclaimed [cite: 73]
